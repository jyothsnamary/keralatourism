KOTTAYAM

Located in the Southwest of Kerala is the district of Kottayam, renowned as the "Aksharanagari" or Land of Letters. Kottayam was the first town in India to achieve total literacy, and about four-fifth of the books published in Kerala, come from Kottayam. Sitting in the basin of Meenachil River and Vembanad lake, Kottayam is home to vast stretches of paddy fields, and massive rubber plantations and spice gardens.
